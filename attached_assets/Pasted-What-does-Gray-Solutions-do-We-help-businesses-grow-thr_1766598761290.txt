What does Gray Solutions do?

We help businesses grow through clear strategy, premium design, and performance-led execution. That includes brand positioning, website and landing page systems, social-first content, ad creatives, and conversion-focused funnels—built to be consistent, measurable, and scalable.

What does a typical timeline look like?

Most projects run in 2–6 weeks, depending on scope.

Week 1: Discovery + goals + audit + direction

Week 2–3: Strategy + copy structure + design system / creative direction

Week 3–5: Production (website, creatives, content, ads, automation)

Week 5–6: QA + launch + tracking + optimisation plan
If you need something faster, we can run a sprint-based delivery.

What do you need from us to start?

To move fast and avoid rework, we usually need:

Your business goal (leads, conversions, awareness, hiring, etc.)

Offer + pricing (or current packages)

Access to existing assets: logo, brand files, website, social handles

Any past performance data: ads, analytics, lead quality notes

One decision-maker for approvals (to keep timelines clean)

How is pricing structured?

Pricing is based on scope + complexity + speed, not “hours burned.”
We work in 3 common models:

Project-based: fixed scope, fixed price (best for websites, launches)

Monthly retainer: ongoing content/ads/optimisation (best for growth)

Sprint packages: short, high-output cycles (best for fast turnaround)
After discovery, you’ll get a clear breakdown of deliverables and cost.

What causes timelines or budgets to change?

Usually one of these:

Scope expands after kickoff (new pages, extra creatives, new features)

Feedback loops take longer than planned (delayed approvals)

New requirements appear mid-way (new brand direction, new offer, new market)

Missing inputs (assets, logins, product details, past data)
We’ll always flag changes early and document them before moving forward.
