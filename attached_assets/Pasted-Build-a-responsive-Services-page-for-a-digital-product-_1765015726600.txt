Build a responsive **Services** page for a digital product & AI studio called **Gray Solutions**.

### Overall layout
- Single scrolling page with sections stacked vertically:
  1. Intro (hero)
  2. Quick brief about us (4 cards)
  3. Road Map (process)
  4. Capabilities (3 columns)
  5. Client Testimonials
- Max content width: 1120–1200px, centered with `margin: 0 auto`.
- Global padding: `padding-inline: 24px` on mobile, `48px` on desktop.
- Use a 12-column feel: clear, aligned grids and consistent gutters.

### Visual & UI design
- Style: clean, minimal, similar to Metalab’s “What we do” page.
- Background: pure white or very light gray (#F7F7F8).
- Text color: dark gray (#111827 or similar).
- Accent color: one strong brand color (for now use #FF6801 for primary CTAs and highlights).
- Typography:
  - Base font: system UI (`-apple-system, system-ui, sans-serif`).
  - Hero heading: 48–56px on desktop, 32–36px on mobile, bold.
  - Section titles: 32px desktop, 24px mobile.
  - Body text: 16px line-height ~1.6.
  - Buttons: 14–16px, medium weight.
- Cards & surfaces:
  - Use subtle borders `1px solid rgba(15,23,42,0.08)` and soft shadows for hover states only.
  - Border-radius: 16–20px on cards, 999px on pill buttons.
- Buttons:
  - Primary: pill button with solid accent background, white text, subtle shadow.
  - Secondary: pill outline button (accent border, transparent/very light background).
  - Hover: slightly brighter background + small `transform: translateY(-1px)` + stronger shadow.
  - Active: darker background + `transform: translateY(0)` + reduced shadow.
- Animations:
  - Use `transition: all 180ms ease-out` on buttons and cards.
  - Card hover: small lift (`translateY(-4px)`), subtle shadow, border-color accent tint.
- Responsive:
  - Mobile breakpoint ~768px.
  - Stack columns vertically with good spacing.
  - Ensure padding doesn’t collapse; maintain at least 24px vertical gap between sections.
